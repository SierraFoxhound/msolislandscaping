YARD OVERHAUL

The first step in overhauling a yard is to design the new layout. This includes deciding on the placement of features such as trees, shrubs, flowers, and hardscaping. It is important to consider the sun exposure, drainage, and overall look and feel that you want to achieve.

Once the design is complete, the next step is to lay out the new yard. This involves marking the placement of features and then removing any existing plants or structures. It is important to be accurate and precise during this step to ensure that the new yard is built correctly.

The final step is to execute the design and layout. This involves installing new plants, hardscaping, and other features. It is important to follow the design carefully and to use high-quality materials to ensure that the new yard will last for many years.
